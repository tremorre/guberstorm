How to kill in a turn with Guberstorm

This page is dedicated to listing out what board states the deck has lethal in. I hope to have these memorized myself eventually, but for now it is also useful for me as a cheat sheet for that.

Basic Kill

8 mana
Guber, Echo, Tutor
Guber, Echo, FB-Echo, Swords/Shift

This is the fewest-cards-in-hand way to win in one turn. Ideally you have built up at least a pledge and ideally a treasure or two when trying to win like this, because getting to 8 lands is slow.

1 mana here is reserved for the final stage. If you have aspie in hand, then 2 mana is needed for it. However, for this final stage a lot of cards can help you get to the win - any mana-positive 3-drop (henceforth MP3).

MP3s include the following most notably: Reflected Echo (a second one), Wretched Bargain, Festered Effluvium*, Axiomatic Shift*, Ripple Effect, Garren, and Get Your Wish*. However, there are a lot of cards that fit the bill (especially in green), including: International Development, Sustainable Investments, Bind Life to Steel*, Temperamental Growth, Work-Safety Violation, and Sola Contraband Playboy.

If we have to tutor out the Guber with Swords/Shift, then this costs 2 more. If we can flash in the guber with Ikurimoko/Ozan/Get Your Wish, or if we can play it and have it survive a turn, then it costs 2 less.

In most cases we will win once we get infinite surveils, but one copy of discern means we can mill ourself until discern is in gy, then surveil until we hit a MP3, draw it, then echo for gub4.

Let's set aside then all the cards which contribute a single treasure-equivalent when played prior to the main turn, or which contribute only as MP3s giving you the mana to play shift/swords for aspie.

All of the following kills can be improved by 1 mana with an unrelated MP3 in hand.

Kill with Prior Pouncer

6 mana
Guber, Pouncer in Play, Echo, Tutor
Guber → Pounce → Echo → FB-Echo → Tutor

Pouncer is uniquely among our early money-saving-spells able to decrease our necessary threshhold by 2. Instead of casting its ability before everything, we can cast it after a guber is out, thus going +2 net with it.

Kill with Wretched Bargain

7 mana
Guber, Echo, Bargain, MP3, Tutor
Guber → Echo → Bargain → MP3 → Echo → Tutor

Bargain is another card which can be used prior to the 3rd guber to ramp, but it's finicky, requiring an MP3 in order to actually convert the mana it gives you into an advantage. This still is an improvement over most MP3s by themselves, which will just spin your wheels at the 2-guber stage.

Kill with Second Echo

7 mana
Guber, 2x Echo, Tutor
Guber → Echo → Echo → Tutor

This is really boring but I need to point it out because every one of these methods matters. Technically this could be any 3-mana cloner, but I'm not sure if the deck can afford more than 4 cloners when copies 5+ cannot get to 3 gubs on their own. A second Gub in hand also accomplishes this.

Kill with Ripple Effect

7 mana
Guber, Echo, Ripple, Tutor
Guber → Echo → Ripple → FB-Echo → Tutor

Three-mana double blink makes Ripple Effect a doubly-effective MP3, which allows it to be useful a stage earlier than the rest of them.
